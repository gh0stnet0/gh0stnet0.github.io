what this is

SIGNAL/VOID is the audio layer of gh0stnet. No tracklist. No schedule. No interruptions. A continuous signal for those who know where to find it.

It runs when it runs. If it's silent, check back. There's no announcement when it comes back online. That's deliberate.

2Pac, Biggie, NWA, Ice Cube, Cypress Hill. The classics. No apologies.
Trap / drill
Kidd Keo, BHAVI, CA7RIEL, YSY A. Spanish and Latin trap with no filter.

Deftones, RATM, Måneskin. When the signal gets heavier without warning.
Everything else
Trip-hop, corridos, Jersey Club, hardbass. No genre is safe. No tracklist. No warning.
